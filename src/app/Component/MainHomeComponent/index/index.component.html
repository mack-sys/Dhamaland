DhammaLand

Dhammaland’s purpose is to spread the teaching of Sammasambuddha and humankind to come out of sufferings.  Dhammaland is a concept of the Bodhisatta (or Bodhisattva), who liberates other fellow beings before liberating oneself.  This is a knowledge offering center to dwell in the Dhammaland and make the world the land of Dhamma (Doctrine). Dhammaland’s purpose is to spread bodhi so that people who can realize and understand it, and dwell in inner-bodhi. Let’s one cultivate one’s mind and heart, and develop one’s inner-bodhi.
 
Our Mission is to teach the doctrines of the sammasambuddha to Buddhist peoples of India, and all human beings of the world. This is another our Mission of our college that to provide tertiary education in Buddhist Studies leading to award Diploma, Bachelor Degrees, Master Degrees of the Buddhist & Pali University of Sri Lanka, and Research Studies.
